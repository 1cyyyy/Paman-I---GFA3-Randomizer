Song Genie

Have you ever wanted to sing but weren't sure what music to choose?

Fear not! For I, the great and mighty, shall aid in your decision.

Simply push the button, and the genre, first letter, and duration of the song will be displayed.

You might be wondering, "Why didn't it just give me the song?" Yes, I can just give you the song, but where's the fun in that?

Some songs last a very long time so, you're not required to sing all of it. But if you want to, feel free to do so. I just wish you'll still be fine by the end of it.
